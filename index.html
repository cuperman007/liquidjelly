And this is how we do it...

Gather

You know your business. We know how to ask you the right questions, so you can tell us about your business. This ensures we can build you the right software first time.

Design

Writing good software isn't hard, but it can take time. Our designs are thoughtful, appropriate and simple. We choose the right technology in the right places and we don't chase fads or fashions.

Build

We use the right tools, at the right time, in the right way. We like to build fast and we like to build frequently. We thrive off early customer engagement. We're happy to be Agile or Rational. Or somewhere in between.

Test

We utilise the latest tools to check and recheck our code on every build. Software is never bug free, so we work hard to track, report and prioritise bugs. Then we squash them.

Deploy

Your software has to be deployed, be it cloud, server or desktop. We'll ensure efficency, scalability and security.

Support

Bespoke software is all about partnership. We don't dump and run. The code we create defines us and we'll stand by it for as long as you need us.

We've been round the block.

Over the past 20 years, we have had the privilege of working with many of the UK's most prestigious companies on a wide variety of projects.

Parcel Tracking

In addition to leading the conversion of the main UKMail website (static to CMS) we also played an integral role in the design and devleopment of the most used feature on the website - the online parcel tracker. This application processes over 1m hits per month, and aggregates information from multiple backend systems.

eCommerce

End-to-end design, development and support of www.ipostparcels.com; an industry leading eCommerce application. The application integrates with PayPal, Barclays, eBay and SmartFocus and processes in excess of £8m revenue annually.

Document Generation

An automated tool to generate technical documentation for a sprawling environment of both managed and legacy systems. The tool iterates and compares multiple products, in multiple versions installed across multiple servers, and outputs a Wiki, that is always up-do-date.
